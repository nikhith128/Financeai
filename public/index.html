Personal Finance, Reimagined

See your money,

FinanceAI is a lightweight finance tracker — real budgets, live savings goals, and automatic spending insights, all running on your own server with your own data.

Dashboard

Everything at a glance

Net balance, monthly income, expenses, and your savings rate — updated the moment you log something. A category breakdown and a 6-month cash flow trend sit right below, so you see the full picture without digging.

Transactions

Find anything in seconds

Search by merchant, note, or amount. Filter by category, account, date range, or type. Every transaction you've ever logged, in one clean, sortable ledger — not buried in a spreadsheet.

Budgets & Goals

Stay ahead of overspending

Set a monthly limit per category and get a clear visual warning before you blow past it. Track savings goals with a live progress ring and a monthly target that updates as you contribute.

Analytics

Insights you didn't have to ask for

Spending leaks, unusual transactions, weekend spending patterns, emergency-fund runway — computed automatically from your real transaction history. No manual report building.

AI Assistant

Ask your money anything

Powered by Gemini, FinanceAI reads your real transaction history and answers in plain English — where your money's leaking, what's unusual this month, and what to do about it. No dashboards to interpret. Just ask.

Your data stays yours

Full backup and restore, built in — export everything to a file anytime, and restore it just as easily. No lock-in.
